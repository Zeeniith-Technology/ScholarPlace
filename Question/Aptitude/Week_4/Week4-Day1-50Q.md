## TYPE 9-10: INTERVIEW SCENARIOS (Q41-Q50)

**Q41-Q45:** Complex scenarios combining 2-3 concepts above

**Q41** Project 400 wd, 20 workers, 20-day timeline. Day 5: lose 5 workers. Day 12: hire 3. Delay?
- **Answer:** Calculate each phase wd, sum, find total days, compare to 20

**Q42** Worker A 1/6, B 1/8. Work 4 days together, A leaves. B continues 6 more days. Total time?
- **Answer:** Combined rate approach or phase approach

**Q43** Efficiency boost timing: 15-day job, 50% boost. Optimal timing day 1, day 8, or day 12?
- **Answer:** Day 1 saves most time (affects full 70%+), day 12 saves least (affects 20%)

**Q44** Three teams with different efficiencies: wage split fairness across phases?
- **Answer:** Worker-days per phase, then wage per wd, allocate

**Q45** Staffing optimization: Current 10w × 30d. Budget for 5 more workers day X. Break-even point?
- **Answer:** Hiring cost vs. time value calculation

**Q46-Q50:** Expert mastery questions requiring synthesis

**Q46** Multi-factor analysis: Workers joining/leaving + efficiency changes mid-project
- **Answer:** Track each phase separately, sum wd, calculate time, identify bottlenecks

**Q47** Interview: "Why isn't delay proportional to worker loss?"
- **Answer:** Sunk costs, remaining smaller percentage, efficiency effects, etc.

**Q48** Real business scenario: Deadline fixed, budget flexible. How to minimize cost?
- **Answer:** Hire late (saves interest) but ensure deadline met (trade-off analysis)

**Q49** Wage fairness with unequal efficiency and variable durations
- **Answer:** (Work done) / (Total work) × Budget, verify accountability

**Q50** MASTERY: Given vague project scenario, ask clarifying questions before solving
- **Answer:** Identify total wd, phases, efficiency changes, wage model, fairness criteria
