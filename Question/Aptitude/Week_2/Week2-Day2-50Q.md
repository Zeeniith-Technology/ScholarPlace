## TYPE 1: SUCCESSIVE PERCENTAGES (Q51-Q55)

**Q51** Value increases 25%, then decreases 20% from new value. Net % change?
- A) NOT 25 - 20 = 5% (additive fallacy)
- B) Multiplier: 1.25 × 0.80 = 1.00 (net 0% change)
- C) Starts 100 → 125 → 100
- D) All above

**Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Explanation:** Successive changes multiply. 1.25 × 0.80 = 1.00 (back to start).

---

**Q52** Investment grows 15%, then 12%, then 8% over 3 years. Total growth?
- A) NOT 15 + 12 + 8 = 35%
- B) Multiplier: 1.15 × 1.12 × 1.08 = 1.39104 ≈ 39.1%
- C) Total growth 39.1% (not 35% due to compounding)
- D) All above

**Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Compound growth over multiple periods.

---

**Q53** Population decreases 5%, then 3%, then 2%. Final population as % of original?
- A) Multiplier: 0.95 × 0.97 × 0.98 = 0.90218
- B) Final = 90.218% of original
- C) Total decrease = 9.782% (NOT 5+3+2 = 10%)
- D) All above

**Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** Successive decreases: each acts on reduced base.

---

**Q54** Price increases 40%, then decreases 40%. Net change?
- A) 1.40 × 0.60 = 0.84
- B) Final = 84% of original (16% loss)
- C) Despite symmetric changes, asymmetric outcome
- D) All above show asymmetry principle

**Answer:** D  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Explanation:** KEY INSIGHT: ±X% is NOT symmetric. (1+x)(1-x) = 1-x².

---

**Q55** INTERVIEW: Three successive discounts: 10%, 20%, 15%. Customer pays what % of original?
- A) Multiplier: 0.90 × 0.80 × 0.85 = 0.612
- B) Pays 61.2% of original
- C) Effective discount = 38.8% (NOT 10+20+15 = 45%)
- D) All above

**Difficulty:** Hard
